# **Soft Ground Business Profile**

## **Company Overview**

**Soft Ground** is a software development consultancy founded in February 2024, specializing in affordable technical solutions for small businesses in Augusta, Georgia. The company operates on a hyper-local model, focusing on face-to-face relationships with businesses in the immediate community.

## **Mission Statement**

Soft Ground exists to solve two critical problems in the tech industry:
1. **Democratizing Technology**: Providing affordable technical solutions to small businesses who traditionally can't access expensive software development services
2. **Breaking the Experience Barrier**: Creating low barrier-to-entry opportunities for aspiring tech professionals, solving the "need experience to get experience" problem

## **Core Value Proposition**

*"Code can build community and communities can build impactful code"*

## **Services & Offerings**

### **Primary Services**
- **Software Consulting**: Custom software solutions for small business challenges
- **AI Implementation**: Leveraging AI agents and agentic coding for business automation
- **Infrastructure Solutions**: Terraform and cloud infrastructure management
- **App Development**: Custom application development tailored to business needs
- **Maintenance Contracts**: Ongoing technical support and system maintenance

### **Specialization Areas**
- Restaurant management systems (e.g., menu management)
- AI-powered lead generation tools
- Business process automation
- Quick-turnaround technical solutions (3-month project timeline preference)

## **Target Market**

### **Primary Customers**
- **Small businesses** in Augusta, Georgia
- Companies with **time-consuming technical challenges** that can be rapidly solved
- Businesses seeking **affordable alternatives** to expensive development firms
- Organizations needing **3-month or shorter project timelines**
- Companies interested in **ongoing maintenance relationships** ($100/month contracts)

### **Ideal Project Profile**
- "Low-hanging technical fruit"
- Problems that are time-intensive for the business but technically straightforward
- Solutions that provide immediate business value
- Opportunities for long-term maintenance relationships

## **Competitive Advantages**

1. **Over-qualification at Affordable Rates**: Microsoft software developer expertise at accessible pricing
2. **Full-time Job Security**: Ability to offer competitive rates due to problem scale
3. **Hyper-local Focus**: Face-to-face relationship building in Augusta market
4. **Rapid Delivery**: 3-month project completion timeline
5. **Community Integration**: Tech Talk Augusta meetup provides networking and talent pipeline
6. **Modern Tech Stack**: AI, agentic coding, and cloud infrastructure expertise
